# Story and Concepts

## mywant Concept
- **Persistent Memory:** `mywant` acts as chap's persistent memory, recording past skill executions so they can be triggered again.
- **Not Autonomous:** chap does not act autonomously. Instead, it re-executes actions that you have already initiated and recorded.
- **Automatic Generation:** Users (you) don't need to manually draft YAML for `mywant`. Chap (the AI) generates the necessary configuration automatically based on its history of successful skill executions.
